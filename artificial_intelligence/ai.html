Artificial Intelligence

Course Overview:

The Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals course is a comprehensive program designed to introduce participants to the exciting world of Artificial Intelligence (AI). This course is suitable for individuals with a strong interest in computer science, mathematics, and data analysis, who wish to understand the underlying principles, techniques, and applications of AI. Over six modules, students will delve into various facets of AI, including machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, and robotics, gaining valuable insights and practical skills to apply AI solutions in real-world scenarios.

Course Duration: 10 weeks (100 hours of instruction and hands-on projects)

Module 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence.

  • Historical perspective and milestones in AI development
  • Ethical considerations and AI's societal impact
  • Overview of AI subfields and applications

Module 2: Mathematics for AI and Machine Learning Foundations

  • Fundamentals of linear algebra and calculus for AI applications
  • Probability and statistics in machine learning
  • Data preprocessing and transformation techniques
  • Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning paradigms
  • Regression and classification algorithms
  • Model evaluation and performance metrics

Module 3: Neural Networks and Deep Learning

  • Understanding artificial neural networks and their architecture
  • Training deep learning models and optimization techniques
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for image recognition
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Tokenization, text preprocessing, and feature extraction
  • Building sentiment analysis and text classification models
  • Sequence-to-sequence models for language translation

Module 4: Robotics and AI Integration

  • Robotics components and AI-powered control systems
  • AI in autonomous vehicles and drones
  • Collaborative robots and human-robot interaction

Module 5: AI in Real-World Applications

  • AI in healthcare for diagnosis and personalized treatment
  • AI-driven recommender systems for e-commerce and entertainment
  • AI in finance for fraud detection and risk assessment

Module 6: Ethical Considerations in AI and Real-World AI Projects

  • Bias and fairness issues in AI algorithms
  • Privacy concerns and data protection in AI applications
  • Ensuring transparency and accountability in AI systems
  • Working on hands-on AI projects in teams
  • Applying AI techniques to real-world datasets
  • Project presentation and feedback sessions

Prerequisites:

This course is suitable for participants with a background in computer science, programming, or a related field. Familiarity with programming languages like Python and basic mathematics (algebra, calculus, and statistics) is recommended but not mandatory. Enthusiasm and a passion for exploring the world of Artificial Intelligence are essential prerequisites.

Assessment and Certification:

Students will be assessed through a combination of quizzes, examinations, and the successful completion of real-world AI projects. Upon meeting the course requirements and demonstrating a solid understanding of AI concepts and applications, students will receive a certificate of completion, signifying their readiness to contribute to the exciting and transformative field of Artificial Intelligence.
